About Dr. Sultan Shaikh

Postdoctoral Researcher | CEO | Environmental Engineer

Professional Journey

Dr. Sultan Shaikh is a postdoctoral researcher and environmental engineer with a Ph.D. in Sustainable Environment from Hamad Bin Khalifa University (Qatar).

His research spans wastewater treatment, resource recovery, aquaculture feed development, and environmental biotechnology. As CEO of Tekgen Technical Institute, Pakistan, he leads national-level training and consultancy efforts focused on Health, Safety, and Environmental (HSE) compliance.

He also collaborates with international institutions like Texas A&M University at Qatar, working on advanced technologies such as 3D microfluidics, membrane systems, and the use of purple non-sulfur bacteria (PNSB) for sustainable bioproduct generation.

My Vision

My vision is to create a dynamic learning environment that integrates academic excellence with real-world application, ensuring students and professionals are equipped with the knowledge and skills to drive positive change.

I aspire to not only contribute to the advancement of research and technology but also to establish my own university, where students can thrive and grow in an ecosystem that nurtures both intellectual curiosity and practical problem-solving.

My Mission

My mission is to bridge the gap between academia and practical knowledge by providing high-quality training and education to candidates, especially in areas I felt were lacking during my own university journey.

I aim to empower students and professionals alike to realize their full potential through tailored training programs that inspire continuous learning and professional growth.
