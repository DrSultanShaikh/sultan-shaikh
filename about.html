About Dr. Sultan Shaikh

Postdoctoral Researcher | CEO | Environmental Engineer

Dr. Sultan Shaikh is a postdoctoral researcher and environmental engineer with a Ph.D. in Sustainable Environment from Hamad Bin Khalifa University (Qatar). His research spans wastewater treatment, resource recovery, aquaculture feed development, and environmental biotechnology.

As CEO of Tekgen Technical Institute, Pakistan, he leads national-level training and consultancy efforts focused on Health, Safety, and Environmental (HSE) compliance. He also collaborates with international institutions like Texas A&M University at Qatar, working on advanced technologies such as 3D microfluidics, membrane systems, and the use of purple non-sulfur bacteria (PNSB) for sustainable bioproduct generation.

Dr. Shaikh is also a certified IEMA trainer and dedicated mentor, actively guiding students and professionals through workshops, digital platforms, and research supervision. His mission is to bridge the gap between academic excellence and real-world environmental solutions.
