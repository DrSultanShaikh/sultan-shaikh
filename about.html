About Me

Dr. Sultan Shaikh is a committed educator, environmental researcher, and civil engineer with a passion for academic excellence, innovation, and mentoring the next generation of professionals.

About Dr. Sultan Shaikh

Dr. Sultan Shaikh is a highly accomplished Environmental Engineer and Researcher with a Ph.D. in Sustainable Environment from Hamad Bin Khalifa University (HBKU), Qatar. He has led international projects focused on bioresource recovery, wastewater treatment, aquaculture feed production, and environmental biotechnology.

As CEO of Tekgen Technical Institute in Pakistan, Dr. Sultan also serves as a post-doctoral researcher at Texas A&M University at Qatar and HBKU, with publications in top journals. His expertise spans 3D microfluidics, membrane technology, life cycle assessment, and resource recovery using purple non-sulfur bacteria (PNSB).

He is also an IEMA-certified trainer and a mentor helping students globally through workshops, YouTube, and community outreach.
