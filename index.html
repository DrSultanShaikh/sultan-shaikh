Welcome to My Official Website

I am Dr. Sultan Shaikh, CEO of Tekgen Technical Institute and a Postdoctoral Researcher at Hamad Bin Khalifa University, Qatar.

With a passion for advancing environmental sustainability and a commitment to fostering safer workplaces, my work spans cutting-edge research in wastewater treatment, resource recovery, and biotechnology. At Tekgen Technical Institute, I lead the development of HSE training programs designed to equip professionals with the knowledge and skills necessary to ensure safety and environmental compliance across industries.

This site offers a glimpse into my academic and professional journey, my research contributions to sustainable development, and the services I provide. Whether you're looking for expert consultancy, advanced training programs, or the latest in environmental research, I'm here to help drive impactful change toward a more sustainable and safer future.

About Me

I'm Dr. Sultan Shaikh, a Postdoctoral Researcher at Hamad Bin Khalifa University in Qatar and Founder of Tekgen Technical Institute in Pakistan.

With a Ph.D. in Sustainable Environment, my work focuses on pioneering solutions in environmental engineering, wastewater treatment, and sustainable development. I have led several international research projects and published in top-tier journals.

Through Tekgen, I am committed to equipping professionals with cutting-edge knowledge in Health, Safety & Environmental (HSE) training, empowering industries with sustainable and safe practices.
