Research

Research Areas of Interest

Environmental Biotechnology

Exploring microbial processes for bioproduct generation from wastewater and waste valorization.

Water and Wastewater Treatment

Advanced technologies including membrane bioreactors, biofilm reactors, and electrochemical systems.

Resource Recovery & Circular Economy

Recovering value-added products like bioplastics, proteins, and bioenergy from waste streams.

Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)

Assessing environmental impacts of emerging technologies for sustainability benchmarking.

Sustainable Aquaculture

Developing protein-rich microbial feed from treated wastewater to promote circular food systems.

3D Microfluidics & Simulation

Simulating porous media and fluid dynamics using microfluidic devices and imaging tools.

Featured Research Projects

Sustainable Aquaculture Feed via Wastewater Biomass

Post-doc at HBKU: Developed aquaculture feed from microbial biomass using PNSB. Focused on protein recovery, LCA, and pilot-scale production.

3D Microfluidics Using MV-LSFM

Research at Texas A&M Qatar: Modeled groundwater flow and contaminant tracking in microfluidics using advanced volumetric imaging.

Membrane Tech for Biomass & 5-ALA

Post-doc at HBKU: Separation and extraction of valuable microbial products via membrane-based systems for sustainable agriculture.

PHA Production from Wastewater

PhD Project at HBKU: Used PSBR biofilm photobioreactors to recover bioplastics (PHAs) from petrochemical effluents in Qatar.
