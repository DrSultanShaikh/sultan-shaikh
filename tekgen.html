Tekgen Technical Institute

Internationally Certified HSE & Technical Training Provider | Empowering Futures Since 2023

Who We Are

Tekgen is a certified and SECP-registered institute headquartered in Hyderabad, Pakistan. Since 2023, we’ve been committed to delivering internationally accredited training in Health, Safety, and Technical fields.

Our certifications are recognized by awarding bodies such as IOSH (UK), AOSH (UK), IQ-OHS (USA), HSI (USA), and ProQual UK Centres. Our mission is to empower professionals with the practical skills and confidence to succeed globally.

Our Vision

To be Pakistan’s leading technical and safety institute with global recognition — raising industry standards through innovation, education, and excellence in HSE training.

Our Mission

To offer internationally recognized programs that equip learners with job-ready skills, ensuring safety, sustainability, and success across multiple industries and disciplines.

Globally Accredited & Recognized

Tekgen Technical Institute is officially certified and affiliated with internationally recognized organizations in health, safety, and vocational training.

IOSH (UK)

Institution of Occupational Safety and Health – UK’s leading chartered safety body.

AOSH (UK)

Awarding Organisation for Compliance and Safety Training worldwide.

IQ-OHS (USA)

International Quality Occupational Health & Safety Certification body – USA.

HSI (USA)

Health & Safety Institute – providing emergency care training certifications in the US.

ProQual UK Centres

Authorized Centres – Safeel & SHEC for ProQual certifications across the UK and GCC.

Safqual (UK)

UK Accredited Centre providing modern compliance & vocational qualifications.

Founder’s Message

Dr. Sultan Shaikh, the founder and CEO of Tekgen, is a globally recognized environmental engineer, academic, and certified safety trainer. With postdoctoral research experience and global exposure, he leads Tekgen with a vision to make international-standard training accessible in Pakistan. His commitment to education, industry relevance, and sustainable development drives the institute’s core mission.
