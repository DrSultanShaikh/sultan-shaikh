Who We Are

Tekgen is a certified and SECP-registered institute headquartered in Hyderabad, Pakistan.

Since 2023, we've been committed to delivering internationally accredited training in Health, Safety, and Technical fields. Our certifications are recognized by awarding bodies such as IOSH (UK), AOSH (UK), IQ-OHS (USA), HSI (USA), and ProQual UK Centres.

Our mission is to empower professionals with the practical skills and confidence to succeed globally.
